Why I choose to do coding?

Posted by Dhruv Upadhyay on 01/01/2018 in Web Dev

Every generation witnesses the beginning of some new technologies which radically change their way of living. For me, it was the rise of Computers. I grew up observing my father work in the programming field where he was involved in the designing, writing, and testing of computer codes. On my visits to his office, I was fascinated to see the industry works and had decided to make a career in this field. Also, I was introduced to the programming languages in school and I enjoyed playing with codes and using different HTML tags to produce different outputs. I still remember the sense of happiness I had when I successfully compiled the code and produced the desired output. To add to this, I have always been fond of computer games and as a kid, was highly curious to know how a particular game operates with a single command of the player and the code behind different game characters. Thus, my interest in the field coupled with a natural inclination for subjects such as math and computer and knowledge gained through school seminars motivated me to pursue Computer Engineering as my undergraduate program.
